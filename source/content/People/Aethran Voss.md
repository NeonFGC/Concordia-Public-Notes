A gifted historian and researcher, Aethran devoted his early years to uncovering the secrets of [[Godsbane]] alongside his close friend [[Caelthir Vaeloras]]. However, this obsession faded and was replaced by another; his love for his wife, [[Lysa Voss|Lysa]], leaving [[Caelthir Vaeloras|Caelthir]] to continue his work alone, though they still kept in touch via letter. In the Harvest of 1290 A.F, [[Lysa Voss|Lysa]] fell pregnant, and Aethran truly believed his life perfect.
Details of what followed this are unknown, but [[Aethran's Notes]] suggest that [[Lysa Voss|Lysa]] and their child perished in mysterious circumstances, and grief consumed Aethran. His studies took a darker turn, and in the ancient ruins beneath [[Helgrind]] he discovered a truth he believes was deliberately hidden: that the gods are not divine saviours, but part of a constructed lie upheld by  [[The Veil of Mercy]]. Driven by obsession he seeks to break that Veil, believing that only by destroying it can the world face the truth, and perhaps reclaim what was lost.
