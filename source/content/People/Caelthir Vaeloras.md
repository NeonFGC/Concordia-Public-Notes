A scholar of noble lineage, Caelthir was the star of modern day [[House Vaeloras]]. He was once a close collaborator and dear friend to [[Aethran Voss]]. Where [[Aethran Voss|Aethran]] persued answers with growing intensity, Caelthir remained measured and cautious, seeking to understand and safeguard their discoveries. His expeditions uncovered the first confirmed fragments of [[Godsbane]], which he ensured were secured within his families vault for study and protection.
