# Into the depths
As the party sail north, they raid the coffers for any valuables, and finding a store of ale they enjoy a drink together, in which [[Tealeaf]] got horribly drunk. A short while later the boat enters the mouth of a dark cavern, the river becoming more and more narrow. Eventually they reach a dead end, where a rockfall blocked the path. Exiting the boat with their hostage, the crew come across a small abandoned camp where they found the [[Letter to Aethran]] from [[Caelthir Vaeloras]]
# The Last Light
Descending further down into the deep, the party begin to hear and see strange apparitions. They make short work of a small pack of Shadows, before entering an ancient tower known as The Last Light. Inside they find the first of [[Aethran's Notes]]. Exploring further they find further notes and an incorporeal form of [[Aethran Voss]], whom [[Quartztopher]] immediately attacks before running off into the darkness. 
# Encounter
As they work their way through the maze that is [[Helgrind]], the party finally stumble upon an exit, blocked by the shadowy ghost of [[Aethran Voss]]. As he attempts to deliver his villainous monologue, he is interrupted by [[Quartztopher]] who Tekken style 10 hit combos him.
The party exit into the night, indicating that the party spent a whole day in [[Helgrind]], meaning [[Bryan]] has been dead for two days.
