# Seraphine!
The group heads north to the ruins, where they hear fighting off in the distance. They discover [[Seraphine]] and some knights of the Pale facing off against a group of cultists led by Ser Corrin Grainward, a once heroic figure who has succumbed to insanity. The group saved [[Seraphine]] as she bade them to enter the ruin, to chase after the cultist who had stolen her [[Godsbane]] relic.
