After being cast from [[Pentara]] by the death of [[Neressa Vhal]] and the now-familiar voice calling them onward, the party awoke far from home, south of a mountain range known as [[Helgrind]]. There, in a dreamlike temple, they were granted a glimpse of the truth by a sibling of [[The Serene - Ishtar, Voice of Still Waters|Ishtar]], who revealed that [[Godsbane]] was forged to bind [[Vel'Kareth]], and that [[The Veil of Mercy]] had been created to erase memory of that ancient evil from mortal minds. Before they awoke, they were left with a single instruction:

"_Seek the Elf_"

The party awoke in a forest clearing, disoriented and far from anywhere they recognised. The land itself proved strange and shifting, with twisted paths and Fey interference leading them astray, culminating in a violent encounter with a family of pixies after [[Quartztopher]] destroyed a fairy ring.

They eventually reached [[Stonebrook]], a village doomed by [[The Black Harvest]], where they agreed to help [[Orimis Vaeloras]] smuggle his family to safety in exchange for a boat. Their escape downriver was interrupted by a blockade from [[The Order of the Pale]], which descended into chaos when druids attacked. In the aftermath, [[Orimis Vaeloras]] was slain, and the party continued on, burdened by their actions.

At [[Larkspur Bay]], they found brief respite before uncovering a basilisk operation run by [[The Silver Web]] and traitors of the Pale. Defeating them, they freed [[Reek Prime]], who granted them ownership of an abandoned keep.

Continuing north, they joined [[Seraphine]] in battle against cultists and pursued them into the ruins of [[The Silent - Korran, Forger of Chains|Korran]]’s tomb. There, they arrived too late to stop a ritual that sacrificed the god [[The Silent - Korran, Forger of Chains|Korran]], breaking [[The Veil of Mercy]].

In the aftermath, [[Bryan]] died.

With only days to revive him, thanks to [[Seraphine]]’s _Gentle Repose_, the party pressed on. Joined by [[Elias]] and after clashing with [[The Gilden Tusks]], they made their way to [[Helgrind]].

Within the mountain, reality twisted. They uncovered the writings of [[Aethran Voss]], revealing the truth behind [[Godsbane]], the gods, and the Veil itself. After navigating the shifting depths and confronting echoes of [[Aethran Voss|Aethran]], they finally escaped.

An entire day had passed.

[[Bryan]] had now been dead for two.
