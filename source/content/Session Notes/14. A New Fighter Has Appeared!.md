# Back Home!
With [[Bryan]] on [[Brokk]]'s back, the party travel through the night back to [[Unnamed Bastion]], to collect their coin and rest up. [[Tealeaf]] attempted to pick up the [[Moonblade]] but was rejected. [[Brokk]] began the construction of some stronger armour for [[Tealeaf]], which [[Reek Prime]] promised to finish before their next return. [[Res]] sent a message to his family with his familiar, and attached a small pouch containing some of the loot they had found.
___
# Elias Appears!
Heading south to [[Larkspur Bay]], the party encounter a man dressed in gladiator garb in the middle of a confrontation with a small group of bandits, of which only one had survived. As the party approach [[Elias]], he picks up his hostage and shows the parties face to him, before sending him on his way. He joins the party, to the reluctance of a few members, and together they continue on their journey.
___ 
# The Tusks
Back at [[Larkspur Bay]], the party head straight to the docks to get their boat, but they notice that the dock keeper is engaged in a heated conversation with a group of Orcs. [[Elias]] recognises them as members of [[The Gilden Tusks]] and persuades the party to attack them. They make short work of the group before taking one of the nearby guards, [[Gred]], hostage. Sailing off north, the party make their way back up the river towards their next destination: [[The Grim Passage]]
