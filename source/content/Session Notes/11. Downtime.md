# Shark Attack!
Travelling south down the river, the party get attacked by a small gang of Sharks, however they quickly manage to scare them off thanks to some sweet talking by [[Quartztopher]]. Before long the boat arrives at [[Larkspur Bay]], a small coastal village south of [[Stonebrook]].

# The Bath Episode!
The party head to a [[The Sober Dwarf ]], a tall, old building owned by the tavern keeper [[Grom]]. Securing a place to stay and a good meal, they heard the number 1 place to visit in town is the [[Larkspur Bath's Inc.]], an ancient hot spring bathhouse recently purchased and renovated by [[Cai]], the travelling merchant who previously sold them the magical cabbages. After being royally ripped off by being charged double compared to the locals, everyone other than [[Bryan]] relaxes in the baths.

# A New Home
Heading north to try and meet up with [[Seraphine]], who has been seen heading to an ancient ruin with a battalion of [[The Order of the Pale]] warriors, and perhaps unknowingly being followed by a group of [[The Order of Chaos]] cultists, the party bump into some bandits of [[The Silver Web]]. Following them back to an abandoned keep they found a horrific sight - The bandits had joined with a couple of traitors of the Pale, and using a Basilisk they are turning the workers of the keep to stone to sell as decoration. The last remaining worker, a Tortle by the name of [[Reek Prime]], is imprisoned in a cage. A battle follows in which the party easily win, and release [[Reek Prime]]. Thankful, he gives ownership of the keep to the party, and agrees to let them use it as their home base going forward.
