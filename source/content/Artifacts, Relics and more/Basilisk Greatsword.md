_Weapon (Greatsword), Uncommon (+1)_
You have a bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The bonus is determined by the weapon’s rarity.
When this weapon hits a creature, that creature must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or take 2d6 Poison damage and have the Poisoned condition for 1 minute.
