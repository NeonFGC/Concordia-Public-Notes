_Light Hammer, Artifact (Requires Attunement)_

A ceremonial hammer found in the tomb of Korran.

**Hammer of the Forge.** This magic Light Hammer grants a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with it. When you hit with an attack using it, the target takes an extra 1d6 fire damage.

**Reforged.** While holding the hammer, you have resistance to fire damage and are immune to exhaustion. Additionally, you have proficiency with smith's tools and have advantage on all ability checks made using them.

**Spells.** While the hammer is on your person, you can use an action to cast one of the following spells (save DC 15): [Heat Metal](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:heat-metal), [Elemental Weapon](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:elemental-weapon), [Fabricate](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:fabricate). Once you use the hammer to cast a spell, a spell can't be cast again from it until the next dawn.
