_Wonderous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement by a Tortle)_
This vibrant array of magical lacquers offers not only striking aesthetic change but also a potent protective charm. It comes in five distinct hues: Red, White, Blue, Green and Black. 

As an action, you can apply a chosen colour to your shell. Along with the visible alteration, the lacquer imbues your shell with a resistance based on the colour used: Red offers resistance to fire damage, white to cold, blue to lightning, green to poison, and black to acid damage.

The lacquer's effect lasts for 24 hours or until you use an action to clean it off or apply a different colour. Each jar contains enough lacquer for 3 applications.
