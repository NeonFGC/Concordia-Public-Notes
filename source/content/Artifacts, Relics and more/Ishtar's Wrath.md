_Weapon (Unique), legendary (requires attunement)_
An ancient relic said to have been forged before the creation of [[index|Concordia]] to embody the three aspects of divine justice: Judgment, Reckoning and Mercy. Each of these forms empower Divine Smite with additional effects.
As a bonus action, you can cause the weapon to transform into one of its three forms.
You can change its form at the start of your turn without using an action.

### Judgement - Morningstar
_1d8 piercing_
**Smite of Judgement** - When using Divine Smite, the target takes an additional 1d8 radiant damage. If the target is large or smaller, it must succeed on a strength saving throw against your spell save DC or be knocked prone.
### Reckoning - Flail
_1d8 bludgeoning_
**Smite of Reckoning** - When using Divine Smite, creatures of your choice within 5 ft of the target must make dexterity saving throws. On a failed save, each creature takes damage equal to half of the damage of the divine smite.
### Mercy - Whip
_1d4 slashing_
**Smite of Mercy** - When using Divine Smite, the target must make a wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC. On a failure, the target is grappled until the start of your next turn.
