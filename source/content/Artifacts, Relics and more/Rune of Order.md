_Wondrous item (rune), Uncommon (Requires attunement by the Gemthane Arm user)_

A hexagonal rune of dark iron veined with gold.

When inserted into the Gemthane Arm, it grants the following effects.

**Magical Strikes.** Your unarmed strikes with the Gemthane Arm ignore resistance to damage.

**Stored Precision.** The rune stores kinetic force with each successful hit. Each time you hit a creature an attack using the arm, gain 1 charge (up to a maximum of 3). When you miss an attack, you lose all stored charges.

You can expend charges to enhance a strike:
**1 charge** - The attack deals an additional 1d6 force damage.
**2 charges** - The attack deals an additional 2d6 force damage, and the target is pushed 5 feet away from you.
**3 charges** - The attack deals an additional 3d6 force damage, and the target must succeed on a strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
