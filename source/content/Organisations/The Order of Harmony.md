The second most prominent religion in [[index|Concordia]], and the main religion of [[Pentara]].
They worship the Goddess [[The Serene - Ishtar, Voice of Still Waters|Ishtar]], and believe that mortals are inherently disharmonious, and as such only pacifism can lighten the way.
